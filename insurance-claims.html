The Process

Step by Step: How Meza General Contractors Handles Your Claim

From first call to new roof — we manage every step.

1
Same / Next Day

Forensic Damage Assessment

Our experts perform a comprehensive digital sweep to document the structural damage adjusters often miss — high-resolution photos, measurements, and a full damage inventory.

2
The NOAA Lock

Meteorological Verification

Data-driven proof: We cross-reference official NOAA storm records with your damage to neutralize insurer arguments about “maintenance neglect.” This documentation is difficult for an insurer to dispute.

3
Critical Step

Strategic Claim Initiation

We provide the forensic evidence package and file the claim on your behalf — positioned as storm damage from the first contact, before any denial track can open.

4
On-Site

On-Site Technical Support

We walk the roof with your adjuster and ensure every detail of our forensic report is identified and included in the scope. Nothing gets minimized or missed.

5
Execution & Enforcement

Approval & Replacement

Claim approved — we mobilize. You cover your Homeowner’s Responsibility Share and we handle the rest. If your valid claim is denied, we can refer you to independent insurance attorneys who specialize in policy enforcement.
