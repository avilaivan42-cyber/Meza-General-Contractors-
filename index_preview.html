The Process

How We Support Your Property Damage Documentation

Our step-by-step approach helps you prepare clearer documentation before the next step.

1

Free Initial Evaluation

We conduct a thorough on-site Initial Evaluation of your roof and property — at no cost, no obligation.

2

Damage Verification

We help you organize and verify your property's damage evidence before your first contact with the insurer. Proper documentation from the start makes a significant difference.

3

Adjuster Walk-Through

Our specialized team attends every adjuster walk-through, making sure every area of damage is properly identified and recorded.

4

Roof Replaced

Documented, scheduled, and replaced by our own crew. Subject to your specific policy terms, your primary cost is typically limited to your Homeowner's Responsibility Share (Deductible). Should a policy gap or variance arise, flexible financing options are available for qualifying homeowners.

Our Approach

Why Clear Documentation Matters

Deep Houston roots, a proper documentation strategy, and a precise understanding of industry-standard property assessments.

1
Proper Timing Matters

We help you compile comprehensive property documentation before your first contact with the insurer. Starting with clear, objective facts helps homeowners make better decisions from the beginning.

On-Site Support

We Stay Until It's Right
