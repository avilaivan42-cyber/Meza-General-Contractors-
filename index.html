The Process

How We Help You Through the Insurance Claim Process

Our step-by-step approach gives your claim the best possible chance of a successful outcome.

1

Free Damage Assessment

We conduct a thorough on-site inspection of your roof and property — at no cost, no obligation.

2

Damage Verification

We help you organize and verify your property's damage evidence before your first contact with the insurer. Proper documentation from the start makes a significant difference.

3

Adjuster Walk-Through

Our specialized team attends every adjuster walk-through, making sure every area of damage is properly identified and recorded.

4

Roof Replaced

Claim approved, roof replaced by our experienced crew. Subject to your specific policy terms, your primary cost is typically limited to your Homeowner's Responsibility Share. Should a policy gap or variance arise, flexible financing options are available for qualifying homeowners.

Our Approach

Why Our Clients Get Better Claim Outcomes

Deep Houston roots, proper documentation strategy, and knowing exactly what insurance adjusters need to see.

1
Proper Timing Matters

We help you prepare your claim documentation before your first contact with your insurer. Starting with objective facts helps homeowners make better decisions from the beginning.

2
NOAA Storm Research

We cross-reference our on-site findings with official NOAA weather history for your neighborhood, giving you a reliable, data-backed timeline of your property's storm history.

3
On-Site Support

Our specialized team attends every adjuster walk-through in person, ensuring nothing gets minimized or missed.

We Stay Until It's Right
