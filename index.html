The 4-Step Process

1

Free Initial Evaluation

We conduct your 100% Free Initial Evaluation to check for storm damage before your first contact with an insurer. This ensures you start with clear, objective physical facts about your roof’s current condition without risking a premature or unnecessary insurance record.

2

Contract Activation & Data Verification

When you hire Meza to build your roof, our team officially activates your project file. At this step, we compile full, heavyweight technical data, pull the official NOAA weather history for your specific neighborhood timeline, and document high-resolution blueprint forensics to build a rock-solid foundation for your construction project.

3

On-Site Physical Support

When you partner with Meza for your roof replacement, our specialized team stands right there with you during the insurance adjuster’s physical walk-through. We present our on-site construction data and physical findings to ensure all structural damage to the building is accurately pointed out and visible to the adjuster.

4

Roof Replaced & Built to Last

We guide you from initial documentation to a finished, high-durability roof engineered to stand up to the Texas elements. Subject to your specific policy terms, your primary out-of-pocket construction cost is usually limited to your Deductible. Should a policy gap or uncovered balance arise, flexible financing options are available for qualifying homeowners.
