Hurricane Harvey · 2017

When Harvey Hit Houston, Meza General Contractors Stayed.

Storm chasers flooded into Houston after Harvey — contractors from out of state with no knowledge of the city, its homes, or its people. They took deposits, filed bad claims, and disappeared.

Meza General Contractors has been in Houston since 1958. We weren't going anywhere. We worked every day through the aftermath — helping provide evidence for claim documentation, meeting adjusters, and replacing roofs for the community that had trusted us for decades.
