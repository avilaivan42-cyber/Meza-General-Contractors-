Roofing Systems

Commercial Systems We Install & Repair

We work with every major commercial roofing system. Our crews are experienced with Houston's humidity and heat loads.

TPO Single-Ply

Thermoplastic polyolefin membrane. Industry-standard for flat and low-slope commercial roofs. Energy-efficient, highly reflective, excellent for Houston heat.

Most Common

Metal Roofing

Standing seam and corrugated metal for commercial applications. 40–50 year lifespan. Ideal for warehouses, industrial facilities, and large retail.

Longest Lifespan

Modified Bitumen

Multi-layer asphalt-based system with excellent waterproofing. Ideal for flat roofs in Houston's heavy rain environment.

Best Waterproofing

Built-Up Roofing (BUR)

Traditional multi-ply system using alternating layers of bitumen and reinforcing fabrics. Extremely durable for heavy-traffic flat roofs.

Heavy Traffic
